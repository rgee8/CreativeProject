Welcome to the World of Geotheda, a World Left in Ruin

Geotheda is a post-apocolyptic world setting that follows what is simply called 'the Calamity'. Now the remains of humanity are struggling to survive. You must unite the last bits of civilization so that humanity may begin to grow once again. However this is easier said then done. There are evil forces that are constantly working against you. Through the chaos a corruption is slowly spreading across the land which will soon consume all you know in darkness.
